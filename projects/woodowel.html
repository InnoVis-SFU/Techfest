WooDowel: Innovative Plywood Sensor

Explore this project with: Yanghao Shi

Project members: Yonghao Shi, Chenzheng Li, Yuning Su, Xing-Dong Yang, Te-Yen Wu

Project Description

We will be demonstrating a smart plywood that acts as a vibration sensor to detect various user activities, such as writing, occurring in contact with the plywood. Woodworkers can use this plywood to create smart furniture or household items.

Most Recent Publication

Shi, Y., Li, C., Su, Y., Yang, X. D., & Wu, T. Y. (2024, May). WooDowel: Electrode Isolation for Electromagnetic Shielding in Triboelectric Plywood Sensors. In Proceedings of the CHI Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems (pp. 1-17).
